PADICO Holding established Al Mashtal Tourism Investment Company in 2000. In 2011, after years of delay due to political instability, the company opened the Mashtal Hotel, which is the first five-star hotel in the Gaza Strip managed by the Spanish hotel operator, Arc Mediterrenin - the Arc of the Mediterranean. . As a result of the conditions prevailing in the sector and the unfavorable security situation, the company terminated its contract with the Spanish company and enabled the hotel to work independently using experienced Palestinian employees.

Despite the political and economic instability in the Gaza Strip, the hotel managed to succeed locally and internationally with hundreds of visitors coming to the Gaza Strip through the border crossing with Egypt. International staff and foreign journalists have also resided in the nursery. The hotel is now equipped to provide all the services required by tourists
